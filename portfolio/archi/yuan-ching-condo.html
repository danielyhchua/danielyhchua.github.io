Yuan Ching Condominium

Winner, Autodesk ASEAN Design Competition 2014/2015

Yuan Ching Road, Singapore

Inspired by the image of a lone tree emerging from the forest canopy, this proposal seeks to design residential towers that rise above the surrounding building heights, creating a landmark for a sleepy sub-urban residential town in Singapore. This proposal aims to improve the quality of living for residents by providing greater access to greenery, minimising energy consumption, and the ample multi-purpose social spaces throughout the block.

Each tower comprises 3-bedroom, 4-bedroom and penthouse units, arranged in a staggered manner vertically, alternating between 3-bedroom and 4-bedroom units, to create a double volume area at the periphery of each 4-bedroom unit. Vertical greenery completes this double volume area as the foliage acts as shading for the interior of the apartments.

The 4-bedroom units and the lower level of each penthouse include a balcony area, allowing residents to enjoy uninterrupted views of the adjacent Jurong Lake. The use of full-length pivot doors at the balcony helps to channel wind into the apartment, minimising the need for air-conditioning in a tropical country.
