Shipwreck

SUTD Capstone Project, 2015

S.E.A. Aquarium, Resorts World Sentosa, Singapore

As part of the expansion of the S.E.A. Aquarium, the master plan of the project called for a theme of ‘Land to Sea’, where visitors transit from ‘Land’ at the Maritime Experiential Museum to ‘Sea’ at the S.E.A. Aquarium.

Situated just outside the entrance to the aquarium, this project draws reference from both its location and the master plan, giving rise to the theme of shipwreck on a beach.

A shipwreck creates a beautiful dialogue between nature and the ship, in which sea animals gradually inhabit through cracks and voids. Conceived as a ‘Beach’, our exhibit serves as a transition between ‘Land’ and ‘Sea’.

[Other members: Chia Zhong Ying, Doron Teh En-Tsien, Kenickie Cher Jia Jun, Kwong Shi Min, Ng Zu Da, Phua Wei Ting Delia, Teo Wan Ting Mavis ]
