Project Lightboard

City College, Singapore

Conceived as a series of short, bite-sized video lessons to help students in memory retention, Project Lightboard is the online education arm of City College. I spearheaded the implementation of Project Lightboard in 2018 and 2019. Besides video lessons for both Primary and Secondary school subjects, quizzes are also being used to scaffold student's learning through the Canvas Learning Management System.
